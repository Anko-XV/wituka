ABOUT

Wituka works in collaboration with talented and independent artists across the world. with the strife of making unique collection of quality clothing at a reasonable price. At Wituka they work by the mantra 1 shirt=1 tree, which co-exists with their goal. Sustainability through sustainable products, sustainable process and sustainable goal. to reach this they collaborate with the eden project, by planting trees and creating new job opportunities.

materials

Wituka also achieves eco-friendlyness by the selection of materials they use. Such as 100% organic cotton with fairtrade and gots certificates. This type of cotton is grown is such a ways that it has less environmental impact. The cotton originates from Bangladesh, but they do printing process in spain.
The swimwear is made with 100% recycled polyster. this material helps reduce 20% of water usage, 50% energy comsumption. and 60 % less co2 emmission. its possible to recycle the already recycled polyester again, making it a very high valued material to use in the circular economy.
