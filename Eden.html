EDEN

wituka is partners with the Eden reforestation project. they have achieved the forest level partnership by contributing over 1,400,000 trees. these have been planted in over 10 diffrent countries and they have also empowered 14.800 employees with fair wages.
For their active help wituka has recieved a certificate, which you can see on their official webisite or on the eden project website.
if you would also like to help against deforestation you can by buying or donating directly toedenprojects.org
